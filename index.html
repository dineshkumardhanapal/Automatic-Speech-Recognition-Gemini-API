Executive Summary

Proscribe is an end-to-end encrypted platform designed to securely capture, transcribe, and analyze audio from clinical interactions. Built entirely on the Microsoft Azure cloud, it addresses the critical need for accurate documentation, quality assurance, and robust security in modern healthcare.

The platform offers real-time transcription, secure 2FA login, and AI-driven analytics to assess communication quality. The goal is to improve clinical efficiency, ensure HIPAA compliance, and provide valuable insights for training and quality improvement, leading to better patient outcomes.

The Challenge in Healthcare Communication

Security & Compliance Risks

Protecting sensitive Patient Health Information (PHI) across various communication channels is complex and critical.

Administrative Burden

Manual transcription and documentation consume valuable organization time, leading to burnout and potential inaccuracies.

Lack of Quality Metrics

It is difficult to objectively assess and provide feedback on communication quality for training and improvement.

Core Feature: End-to-End Security

Encryption in Transit

All data secured with TLS 1.3 between the user and Azure services, protecting data on the move.

Encryption at Rest

Data in Azure Blob Storage and SQL is encrypted, with Customer-Managed Keys in Azure Key Vault for ultimate control.

Secure Authentication

Microsoft Entra ID B2C provides robust identity management with mandatory Two-Factor Authentication (2FA).

All selected Azure services are HIPAA-eligible, and Microsoft provides a Business Associate Agreement (BAA).

Core Feature: Live Transcription & Diarization

Real-Time Clinical Information

The platform displays streaming captions live during Insurance call between a representative and a caller, providing immediate feedback and insights.

Speaker Diarization automatically identifies and labels who is speaking, creating a clear, easy-to-read record of the entire interaction.

Speaker 1 (Rep): Hi, Thank you for Calling Humana Insurance, My name is Lia.

Speaker 2 (Caller): Hi Lia this is Peter, Calling from Medical Facility to Check Members Eligibility & Benefits

Speaker 1 (Rep): Ok, Can I get the Provider NPI or Tax ID

Core Feature: Advanced Quality Metrics

Pronunciation Score

Azure AI Speech provides scores on accuracy, fluency, and identifies mispronounced words for targeted feedback.

Conversational Ability

Azure AI Language analyzes sentiment and tone to gauge the quality of the interaction and patient rapport.

Protocol Adherence

A custom module checks if key phrases for proper call introductions and conclusions were used.

Result: An objective, data-driven score for training and quality improvement.
